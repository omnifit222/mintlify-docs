## **What is the Password Section?**

The Password section in your OmnifitAI dashboard allows you to manage and update your account password securely. This section is part of the My Account settings and ensures that only you have control over your account credentials. It also provides the option to cancel any changes or delete your account, if needed.

---

## **Benefits of the Password Section**

- **Secure Account Access**: Regularly updating your password helps protect your account from unauthorized access.
- **Full Control**: You decide when and how to change your password directly from the dashboard.
- **Quick Edits**: Change your password in just a few clicks without any external support.

---

## **Steps to Change the Password of your OmnifitAI Account**

1. Log into your OmnifitAI Account
2. Once logged in, locate the left-hand sidebar. Scroll to the bottom and click on the **"More"** option found below the **Help Center** section.
3. In the expanded **More** section, click on **"My Account"**, which appears just above the **Logout** option.
4. Inside the **My Account** section, you will see two tabs at the top. Click on the second tab labeled **"Password"**.
5. On the Password screen, first enter your current password. Then, type in your new password, followed by confirming the new password. After verifying the details, click on **“Update”** to save your new password.
6. If you decide not to proceed, you can click **“Cancel”** to discard the changes. Alternatively, you will also find the option to **Delete Your Account** at the bottom of the page if you wish to permanently remove your profile.

Congratulations! You have just used the "Password" section of the accounts section.
