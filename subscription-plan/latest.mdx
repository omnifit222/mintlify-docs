## Platform Subscriptions

The platform offers a flexible, SaaS-based subscription model that allows you to scale usage based on your operational needs. You can manage your subscription, message limits, and add-ons directly from the Dashboard.

Subscriptions are structured around **pricing plans** and **message add-ons**, giving you granular control over monthly or yearly consumption.

---

## Current Subscription

You can review your active subscription details from the **Subscriptions** section within the Dashboard.

**How to access:**

- Navigate to the left-side menu
- Click on **Subscriptions**
- Open the **Subscriptions** tab

Here, you will be able to view:

- Your current plan
- Billing cycle (Monthly / Yearly)
- Message limits
- Active add-ons
- Renewal status

### Starter Plus Plan

- **Price:** \$99 / Month
- **Messages:** 5,000
- **Chatbots:** 4
- **Human Agents:** 1

### Growth Plan

- **Price:** \$199 / Month
- **Messages:** 12,000
- **Chatbots:** 12
- **Human Agents:** 4

### Pro Plan

- **Price:** \$399 / Month

## Upgrade Your Plan

Upgrading your plan is a streamlined, self-serve process.

1. Navigate to **Subscriptions** from the left menu and open the **Plan** tab.

2. On the plans screen:
   1. Select billing frequency: choose between **Monthly** or **Yearly** billing using the toggle.
   2. Review available plans: Compare plan limits such as message volume, chatbot count, and human agent allocation.
   3. Select a plan: Click on the **Change Plan** button under the desired plan.
3. Follow the on-screen checkout instructions to complete payment.

Once payment is successful, your new plan is activated immediately, and updated limits apply from the same billing cycle.

## Downgrade or Cancel Subscription

If you need to reduce usage or pause billing, you can downgrade your plan at any time.

1. Go to **Subscriptions** → **Plan** tab from the Dashboard.
2. Select the **Free / Lower Plan** option available under your current plan.
3. Confirm the downgrade request in the confirmation modal by clicking **Downgrade**.
4. Once confirmed:
   1. The downgrade request will be queued
   2. Your existing plan remains active until the end of the billing cycle
   3. No further charges will apply after the cycle ends

You can track the downgrade status directly from the **Subscriptions** tab.
